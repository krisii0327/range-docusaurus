# About CodeTechSolutions

CodeTechSolutions is a leading AI solutions and management platform company founded in 2018 and headquartered in Budapest, Hungary. With approximately 25 talented professionals across Development, Operations, Sales, HR, Finance, Helpdesk, and PMO, we specialize in delivering AI-powered enterprise solutions and intelligent management platforms to enterprise clients.

Our expertise spans AI-driven analytics platforms, intelligent management systems, and automated workflow solutions. We serve key clients in the banking, insurance, healthcare, and manufacturing sectors, helping them harness artificial intelligence to modernize their operations and accelerate digital transformation initiatives.

## Our Mission

We're committed to building AI solutions that don't just meet today's requirements but anticipate tomorrow's challenges. Our intelligent platforms combine cutting-edge machine learning with deep industry knowledge, enabling our clients to stay ahead in rapidly evolving markets.

## Company Values

**Innovation** drives everything we do. We embrace emerging AI technologies and methodologies to solve complex business problems with intelligent, scalable solutions.

**Quality** is non-negotiible. Every algorithm, every platform feature, and every client interaction reflects our commitment to excellence in AI development.

**Collaboration** powers our success. We believe the best AI solutions emerge when diverse perspectives come together, whether within our team or in partnership with our clients.

## Technology Excellence

Our engineering teams work with modern AI/ML stacks including cloud-native architectures, machine learning pipelines, and intelligent automation frameworks. We maintain high standards for model accuracy, platform security, and performance across all our AI-powered projects.

Based in the heart of Central Europe's growing tech ecosystem, CodeTechSolutions continues to grow while maintaining the agility and innovation focus that has defined us since our founding.
