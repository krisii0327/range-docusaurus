## About CodeTechSolutions

Founded in 2018 and headquartered in Budapest, Hungary, CodeTechSolutions has grown to become a trusted partner for AI-powered enterprise solutions. With approximately 25 talented professionals, we specialize in:

- **AI-Powered Analytics Platforms** - Intelligent data analysis that drives business insights
- **Management Platform Solutions** - Smart systems that optimize enterprise operations
- **Automated Workflow Solutions** - AI-driven process automation for maximum efficiency
