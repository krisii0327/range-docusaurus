# Project Portfolio

CodeTechSolutions currently manages 6 active projects across enterprise software, cloud solutions, and fintech applications. Our project portfolio represents diverse technical challenges and showcases our expertise in building scalable, secure software solutions.

## Our Development Methodology

### Agile/Scrum Framework
We follow Agile development principles with Scrum methodology to ensure iterative delivery and continuous client feedback:

- **2-week sprints** with regular demos and retrospectives
- **Daily standups** to maintain team alignment and identify blockers
- **Sprint planning** sessions involving product owners and development teams
- **Continuous integration** and automated testing throughout development

### Project Lifecycle
Every project follows our proven lifecycle:

1. **Discovery Phase** - Requirements gathering, technical architecture planning
2. **MVP Development** - Core functionality delivery in 6-8 weeks
3. **Iterative Enhancement** - Feature additions based on user feedback
4. **Production Deployment** - Staged rollout with monitoring and support
5. **Maintenance & Evolution** - Ongoing support and feature development

### Quality Assurance
- **Code reviews** for all changes before deployment
- **Automated testing** including unit, integration, and end-to-end tests
- **Security audits** and penetration testing for all applications
- **Performance monitoring** and optimization throughout development
