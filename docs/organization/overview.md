# Company Overview

CodeTechSolutions is a dynamic AI technology company that transforms how businesses operate through innovative artificial intelligence solutions and intelligent management platforms. Since our founding in 2018, we've built a reputation for delivering enterprise-grade AI applications that solve real-world problems.

## Our Story

What started as a small team of passionate AI developers in Budapest has grown into a thriving company of approximately 25 professionals. We've maintained our startup agility while building the expertise and processes needed to serve enterprise clients across Europe with cutting-edge AI solutions.

Our journey began with a simple belief: artificial intelligence should make business operations smarter, not more complicated. This philosophy continues to guide every AI project we take on and every intelligent platform we build.

## Leadership

**Dr. Balázs Kovács** serves as our CEO, bringing deep expertise in artificial intelligence and enterprise software development. Under his leadership, CodeTechSolutions has established itself as a trusted partner for AI-powered business transformation.

## Mission Statement

**We empower businesses to achieve their full potential through thoughtfully designed AI solutions and intelligent management platforms that scale with their growth.**

Our mission drives us to:
- Build AI systems that solve genuine business challenges
- Create intelligent platforms that grow with our clients' needs
- Maintain the highest standards of security and reliability in AI development
- Foster long-term partnerships based on trust and measurable AI results

## Core Values

### Innovation with Purpose
We don't innovate for innovation's sake. Every AI technology we adopt and every intelligent solution we develop serves a clear business purpose for our clients.

### Quality First
We believe in doing things right the first time. Our commitment to quality shows in our AI models, our platform architecture, and our client relationships.

### Transparency
Open communication builds trust. We keep our clients informed about AI capabilities and limitations, our teams aligned, and our processes clear and documented.

### Continuous Learning
AI technology evolves rapidly, and so do we. We invest in our team's growth and stay current with the latest machine learning best practices.

### Collaboration
The best AI solutions emerge when diverse perspectives come together. We foster collaboration within our teams and with our clients to build truly intelligent systems.
